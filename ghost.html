Girls Hostel of GPT Masab Tank

Welcome to the Girls Hostel of Government Polytechnic Masab Tank, Hyderabad. Our hostel provides a safe and comfortable living environment for female students pursuing their education at GPT.

Located within the campus, our hostel offers modern amenities and facilities to ensure the well-being and convenience of our residents. From spacious rooms to recreational areas, we strive to create a conducive atmosphere for academic and personal growth.

Our dedicated staff and hostel management team are committed to providing support and assistance to students throughout their stay. Whether it's academic guidance, medical assistance, or recreational activities, we aim to cater to the diverse needs of our residents.

**Facilities:** Our hostel facilities include comfortable accommodation, 24/7 security, dining hall with nutritious meals, recreational spaces, study rooms, laundry facilities, and Wi-Fi connectivity.
