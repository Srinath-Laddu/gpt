SBTET Office

Welcome to the SBTET Office of Government Polytechnic Masab Tank, Hyderabad. Our office serves as the administrative hub for the State Board of Technical Education and Training (SBTET) in the region.

The SBTET Office plays a crucial role in the management and regulation of technical education programs offered by polytechnic colleges in Telangana. We oversee curriculum development, examinations, certification, and other administrative functions related to technical education.

Our dedicated team of administrators and staff are committed to ensuring the quality and integrity of technical education in the state. We strive to provide efficient services to students, faculty, and institutions, while upholding the standards set by the SBTET.

**Services:** The SBTET Office offers a range of services including academic counseling, examination scheduling, certification, accreditation, and more. We also facilitate communication between polytechnic colleges, government agencies, and industry partners to promote collaboration and innovation in technical education.
